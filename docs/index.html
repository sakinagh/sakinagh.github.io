About

Founded in 1996 by Ahid Ghafoor, Innovative Design Build has established itself as a leader in delivering innovative solutions with a personalized touch. We specialize in creating high-quality designs that not only transform spaces but also bring ideas to life.

As a minority HUB-certified, full-service Design/Build General Contractor in the Dallas-Fort Worth area, Innovative Design Build focuses on residential, commercial, rehabilitation, and construction projects. We proudly extend our expertise to clients across the state of Texas.

Our primary goal is to ensure 100% customer satisfaction by fostering strong partnerships with local tradesmen, industry professionals, and field experts. With a skilled project management team, we build lasting relationships with clients, contractors, and subcontractors.
